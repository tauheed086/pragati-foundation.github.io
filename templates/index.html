Welcome to Pragati Foundation

Who we are

The Pragati Foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of disabled individuals by providing essential mobility aids like prosthetic legs, walking sticks, and wheelchairs. In partnership with the government, the foundation also helps organize small stalls where disabled individuals can sell goods and earn a livelihood. Through these efforts, Pragati empowers people with disabilities to lead independent, dignified lives.
