Our Story

Who we are

The Pragati Foundation is a beacon of hope and empowerment for disabled individuals, dedicated to promoting fairness and dignity in their lives. This charitable organization focuses on improving the quality of life for people with disabilities by providing essential mobility aids such as prosthetic legs, walking sticks, wheelchairs, and many other tools to enhance their independence and mobility.


Understanding that true empowerment goes beyond just providing physical assistance, the Pragati Foundation also works closely with government bodies to offer economic opportunities to disabled individuals. One of their key initiatives involves setting up small stalls at government-organized events and markets. These stalls allow people with disabilities to sell handmade products, crafts, or services, giving them the chance to earn an income, develop a sense of self-reliance, and build a sustainable livelihood.


Through this multi-faceted approach, Pragati Foundation ensures that disabled people not only have access to essential physical support but are also integrated into society with dignity and respect, fostering an environment where they can thrive and live fulfilling lives.

Our Mission

Empowering Abilities, Enhancing Lives

  • Provide mobility aids like prosthetic legs, walking sticks, and wheelchairs to disabled individuals.
  • Collaborate with the government to create income opportunities for people with disabilities.
  • Promote independence, dignity, and self-reliance among the disabled community.
  • Support the integration of disabled individuals into society through employment.
Our Vission

An Inclusive World for Every Ability

  • A society where disabled individuals have equal opportunities to thrive.
  • A world where disabilities don't limit anyone's potential.
  • An inclusive community where dignity and economic empowerment are accessible to all.

Meet Our Leaders

Vikram Chavan

Founder

Vikram Chavan is a dedicated visionary focused on empowering underserved communities and promoting skill development. Through his leadership with Pragati Foundation, he has pioneered initiatives that uplift the lives of disabled individuals and economically disadvantaged groups, providing them with essential resources, training, and opportunities for self-sustenance.

"Together we can build a bright future"


Anand Bankar

Founder

As the Secretary of Pragati Foundation, Anand Bankar is a dedicated social worker who actively champions the welfare of underprivileged communities. With a compassionate approach, he plays a vital role in organizing initiatives that provide essential resources, vocational training, and community support to individuals in need. His commitment to social justice and community upliftment is central to the Foundation's mission, inspiring positive change and resilience among those it serves.

"In service of hope and resilience."
